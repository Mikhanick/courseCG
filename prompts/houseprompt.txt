## Critical Geometry Rules
- **Coordinate System:**
  - X-axis: Building width (0 = left edge, max = right edge)
  - Y-axis: Height (0 = ground level)
  - Z-axis: Building depth (0 = street-facing facade, max = back wall)
- **Vertex Requirements:**
  - Minimum 8 vertices for rectangular buildings (4 bottom + 4 top)
  - Roof must have at least 5 vertices for pyramidal shapes
- **Face Requirements:**
  - Walls must connect bottom and top vertices (e.g., [0,1,5] for front wall)
  - Roof faces must converge to peak vertex(es)
  - Ground faces (Y=0) are OPTIONAL (system automatically adds terrain contact)

## Color System
**Walls (main surfaces):**
- Residential: `#8B7D6B` (beige), `#A0522D` (brown), `#4682B4` (steel blue)
- Commercial: `#5F9EA0` (cadet blue), `#2F4F4F` (dark slate gray)
- Industrial: `#696969` (dim gray), `#708090` (slate gray)

**Roofs:**
- Flat roofs: `#A9A9A9` (dark gray)
- Sloped roofs: `#808080` (gray), `#8B4513` (saddle brown)
- Modern roofs: `#483D8B` (dark slate blue)

**Windows (detail surfaces):**
- Residential: `#B0C4DE` (light steel blue)
- Office: `#87CEFA` (light sky blue)
- Night view: `#FFD700` (gold)
